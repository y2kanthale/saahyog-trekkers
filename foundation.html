S.A.A.H.Y.O.G. FOUNDATION

Support • Adventure • Altruism • Humanity • Youth • Outreach • Growth

Vision:
To build a compassionate, youth-led movement that combines adventure, service, and sustainable outreach to uplift the lives of the elderly, orphaned children, and underprivileged families across Maharashtra and India.

Our Core Focus (2025–26)

1. Support to Old Age Homes
  • Monthly events, health camps, emotional bonding activities
  • Infrastructure or medicine donation programs
  • Festival celebrations with community participation
2. Support for Orphanages & Children in Need
  • Learning materials, hygiene kits, mentorship drives
  • Summer camps & educational adventure trips
  • Birthday Buddy initiatives (volunteers sponsor a child’s birthday/month)
3. Education Upliftment for Financially Challenged Families
  • Scholarships, digital learning tools, career counseling
  • Skill-based weekend workshops (spoken English, tech basics, art)
  • "1 Youth = 1 Student" mentorship movement
